Why We Win

Four moats no one

VC-funded EdTech from Lagos or Nairobi cannot replicate what EduBloom has built from Abeokuta.

01

Agent Relationships

Principals trust the person who walked into their school. Our agents are embedded in communities across Ogun State and growing. This distribution takes years to build — you can't buy it with Series A money.

02

Offline-First Architecture

Built for zero internet from day one. Works on a ₦30,000 Tecno phone with 2GB RAM. No competitor has done this — they all assumed urban Lagos connectivity that 80% of Nigerian schools don't have.

03

Data Depth — The Vector Lake

17 Firestore collections per school. Every score, every payment, every attendance record, every learning session — timestamped, isolated, schema-stable. At 2,000 schools this becomes Africa's most valuable education dataset.

04

Aari — African AI, Not Adapted Western AI

GPT was trained on the internet. The internet doesn't reflect Nigerian JSS2 maths. Aari will be the first AI trained exclusively on African classroom data — built school by school, term by term, from inside the classroom.
